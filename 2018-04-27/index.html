Why am I using Neocities?


Author: Lionirdeadman
Published: 2018-04-27 · 1 min read
License: Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International.

Well, for one, it's free. "Okay, and?", you may be saying. I don't have the burden of hosting. I can simply code away the html and not worry about anything else..

"This gives me freedom and peace of mind."

Not only that but since there's no database or anything, just static files. I've also further restricted myself by not using javascript (although that may change)

Why? Because the web is bloated and it takes way too long to load most pages and they're just filled with ads, trackers, javascript and other crap.

So I'm just gonna post from here from time to time some decently-sized posts.. You can also subsribe to the RSS to see whenever I post something on here without making an account and stuff.
